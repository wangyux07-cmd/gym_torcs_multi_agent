About

This blog documents a solo TORCS AI racing project. The work explores several approaches to autonomous racing in simulation, including rule-based control, Gymnasium-style reinforcement learning environments, PPO experiments, speed profiles, residual reinforcement learning, sector-level SAC, and a simple web interface for running race demos.

The goal of the blog is to make the project journey understandable: not only the final result, but also the experiments, bugs, design decisions, and technical tradeoffs that shaped the system.
